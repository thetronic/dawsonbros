    • Andrew, Steve and Tim - the Dawson Bros. - are comedy writers who have worked on a variety of narrative, sketch and entertainment shows. Notable credits include two series of hit BBC1 sitcom Big School, co-written with David Walliams, the multi-award winning Ant & Dec's Saturday Night Takeaway, the Banff-nominated sketch show Dawson Bros. Funtime, the BAFTA nominated The Peter Serafinowicz Show, the BAFTA winning That Mitchell And Webb Look and The One Ronnie - their 'Blackberry Sketch' was the BBC's most-watched YouTube clip for more than three years. In 2014 they successfully sold a pilot script to Warner Bros. and NBC in the USA.

    They've also written on The Jonathan Ross Show, Comic Relief's Red Nose Day (including the Stephen Hawking 'Little Britain' sketch, 'Behind the Bond' and 'Simon Cowell's Wedding'), six series of Total Wipeout and were resident writers for Funny or Die UK. They've written for several high profile live events (Royal Variety Performance, BRIT Awards, MTV EMAs, NME Awards), produced/directed music videos for Groove Armada and edited three comedy books including 2011's top-ten bestselling 'The Inbetweeners Yearbook'.
